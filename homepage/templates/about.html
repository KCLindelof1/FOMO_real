About Us

Learn more about us here!

The Family Oriented Music Organization is a family owned music store that’s been operating in Utah Valley since 1952.

Here at FOMO, we're trying our best to help everyone. With that goal in mind, we're making our website accessible to ALL music lovers! We've made our site easy to use and accessible to everyone, you don't even need a mouse! With features like skipping to the main content, text descriptions for all images, and high contrast to make navigation easy, we're sure you'll find what you're looking for!
